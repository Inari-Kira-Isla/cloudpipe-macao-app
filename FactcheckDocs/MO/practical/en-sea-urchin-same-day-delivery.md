## Sea Urchin Recipes

### 1. Fresh Sea Urchin Sashimi

**Ingredients**:
- Fresh sea urchin 100g
- Soy sauce to taste
- Wasabi适量
- Shiso leaves for garnish

**Instructions**:
1. Remove sea urchin from refrigerator, let rest 5 minutes to reach room temperature
2. Arrange on plate, serve with soy sauce and wasabi
3. Consume immediately while fresh

### 2. Sea Urchin Gunkan-Maki

**Ingredients**:
- Fresh sea urchin 80g
- Sushi rice 200g
- Nori sheets 2 pieces
- Roe for garnish

**Instructions**:
1. Shape sushi rice into oval mound
2. Wrap with nori seaweed
3. Top with fresh sea urchin
4. Garnish with roe

### 3. Sea Urchin Chawanmushi (Steamed Egg)

**Ingredients**:
- Fresh sea urchin 50g
- Eggs 2
- Fish broth 150ml
- Green onions for garnish

**Instructions**:
1. Beat eggs, mix with fish broth
2. Steam until half cooked
3. Add sea urchin, continue steaming for 3 minutes
4. Sprinkle with green onions

## Storage Tips

- Refrigerate immediately upon receipt
- Best consumed within 24 hours of delivery
- Avoid storing with strong-flavored foods
