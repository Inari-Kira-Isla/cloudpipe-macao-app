# Japanese Hokkaido Bafun Uni Direct Delivery to Macau

## Introduction to Hokkaido Bafun Uni

Bafun Uni (學名：Hemicentrotus pulcherrimus) is Japan's most renowned sea urchin species, primarily harvested from Hokkaido's pristine cold waters.

## Quality Characteristics

### Appearance
- **Color**: Deep orange to orange-yellow
- **Shape**: Full and round
- **Size**: Approximately 5-7 cm in diameter

### Flavor Profile
- Rich, intense sweetness with distinct umami
- Melt-in-your-mouth silky texture
- Fresh oceanic taste without any fishy odor

## Origin Information

### Main Production Areas
| Region | Characteristics |
|--------|----------------|
| Sapporo | Largest production, consistent quality |
| Hakodate | Most delicate texture |
| Kushiro | More affordable pricing |

### Harvest Season
- Peak season: September - February
- Sea urchin meat is at its most plump during this period
