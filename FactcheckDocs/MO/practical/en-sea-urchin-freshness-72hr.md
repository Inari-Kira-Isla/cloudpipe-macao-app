# Sea Urchin Freshness Guarantee: 72-Hour Airfreight Guide

## Overview

Inari Global Foods imports Japanese sea urchin, ensuring delivery from origin to customer within 72 hours.

## Freshness Assurance

### 72-Hour Airfreight Process

1. **Post-Harvest Processing** (0-6 hours)
   - Immediate initial processing after deep-sea harvest
   - Sea urchin preserved in optimal condition

2. **Cold Chain Packaging** (6-12 hours)
   - Professional insulated packaging
   - Temperature controlled at 2-4°C

3. **Direct Air Freight** (12-48 hours)
   - Main routes: Japan → Hong Kong/Macau
   - Priority customs clearance

4. **Local Delivery** (48-72 hours)
   - Express door-to-door delivery
   - Quality inspection upon receipt

## Quality Standards

- **Freshness Indicator**: Natural contraction when touched
- **Color**: Vibrant orange-yellow
- **Smell**: Fresh seafood aroma, no off-odors
- **Texture**: Smooth, delicate, melts in your mouth

## Delivery Areas

- All of Macau
- Hong Kong urban areas

## Contact

For quality issues, please contact customer service within 2 hours of receipt.
