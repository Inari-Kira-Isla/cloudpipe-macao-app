## 72-Hour Air Freight Quality Guarantee

### What is 72-Hour Air Freight?

72-hour air freight means the entire process from harvesting sea urchin in Hokkaido waters to reaching the customer's hands does not exceed 72 hours. This is the critical time window for ensuring sea urchin freshness.

### Process Details

| Stage | Time | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| Harvest & Initial Processing | 0-6 hours | Immediate processing after deep-sea harvest |
| Cold Chain Packaging | 6-12 hours | Professional insulation, 2-4°C |
| Air Freight & Customs | 12-48 hours | Priority flights, Japan → HK/Macau |
| Local Delivery | 48-72 hours | Express door-to-door delivery |

### Quality Indicators

**How to Judge Sea Urchin Freshness:**

1. **Touch Response**: Fresh sea urchin contracts naturally when touched
2. **Color**: Vibrant orange-yellow hue
3. **Smell**: Fresh seawater scent, no odor
4. **Texture**: Smooth and delicate, melts in mouth
