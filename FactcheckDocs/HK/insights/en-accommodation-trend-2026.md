1. **Mobile Payment Adoption**
   - Alipay, WeChat Pay coverage exceeds 95%
   - Contactless ordering becomes standard

2. **Food Delivery Platforms Boom**
   - Ele.me, Meituan dominate market
   - Restaurant-owned mini-programs emerging

3. **Member Systems & Data Analytics**
   - CRM systems integrated with member消费 data
   - Personalized recommendations boost repeat purchases

4. **Smart Kitchen Equipment**
   - IoT kitchen devices improve efficiency
   - Reduce human error

- High digitalization costs for small-medium restaurants
- Talent shortage
- System compatibility issues

### Hong Kong F&B Industry Facing Market Challenges

#### 2026 Market Status

1. **Rental Pressure**
   - Core area rentals remain high
   - Forces restaurants to downsize

2. **Labor Shortage**
   - Imported labor policy impacts
   - Recruitment difficulty in F&B sector

3. **Changing Consumer Behavior**
   - Local consumption downgrade
   - Tourist consumption patterns shift

4. **Intensifying Competition**
   - Mainland China brands entering Hong Kong
   - Price wars continue
